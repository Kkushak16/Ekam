Privacy Policy: Ekam Secure Chat Platform

Effective Date: July 9, 2026 | Last Updated: July 9, 2026

At Ekam Chat Platform, we are committed to defending our users' privacy and securing their personal communications. This Privacy Policy details the data schemas we capture, our operational storage pipelines, and your security rights under international privacy standards.

1. Data We Collect & Store

To operate our real-time messenger workspace, we gather minimal identifier records necessary to establish secure auth connections:

  • Account Identifiers: We collect display names, email addresses, phone numbers, and username strings during sign-up to map credentials in Supabase PostgreSQL tables.
  • Conversation Content: Chat texts and room memberships are cached in a secure MongoDB cluster to allow real-time message history synch across sessions.
  • Device Metadata: We collect minimal browser details (like user agents and connection logs) to troubleshoot push notification delivery and monitor active WebSockets.

2. How Your Data is Secured

Security is the foundation of the Ekam platform. We employ standard technical measures to safeguard your information:

A. End-to-End Cryptography

All data transfers are conducted using TLS 1.3/1.2 protocols. Session authentication relies on cryptographically signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT) using secure signing keys.

B. Database Isolation

User credentials and password hashes are strictly managed via industry-standard hashing algorithms (bcryptjs), preventing plaintext representation in any of our databases.

3. Compliance & Rights

We comply with international data regulations including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Under these frameworks, users retain rights to request:

  • Access to full exported archives of their messaging history.
  • Immediate deletion or modification of their account profiles.
  • Revocation of active web push notification subscriptions.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or security concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our privacy compliance officer directly at security@ekam-woad.vercel.app.
